Care Track - Healthcare Patient Management System

Healthcare providers face challenges in managing vast amounts of semi-structured patient data—ranging from personal details to medical history, prescriptions, and doctor notes. Care Track is designed as a MongoDB-based Patient Management System that simplifies storing, retrieving, and managing healthcare records efficiently. This project leverages MongoDB's NoSQL flexibility to handle heterogeneous medical data and provides optimized operations for adding, updating, searching, and deleting patient records.
